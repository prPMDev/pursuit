# Scanner Examples

Real and sample scanner evaluations showing the full flow: profile → batch → filtered output.

---

## Available Examples

### `sample-profile.md`
A filled-in profile for "Alex" — a Senior PM specializing in integrations and APIs at B2B SaaS companies. Shows the depth and specificity the profile template expects.

### `sample-batch-input.md`
A batch of 8 job listings in the standardized format the scanner prompt expects. Mix of good matches, borderline, and clear skips.

### `sample-scanner-output.md`
What the scanner prompt produces when given Alex's profile + the sample batch. Shows the summary table, EVALUATE reasoning, MAYBE notes, and stats.

## Contributing Examples

If you use the Scanner and find it valuable, share your examples:

1. **Fill in your profile** (anonymize personal details if needed)
2. **Run a real batch** through the scanner prompt
3. **Save the output** with your assessment of accuracy
4. **Note outcomes** — did you actually pursue the EVALUATE listings? Were they good leads?

### Format

Name your file: `YYYY-MM-[description].md`

Example: `2026-03-senior-pm-integrations-batch.md`

### Anonymization

If sharing real examples:
- Company names: OK to keep or anonymize ("Company A")
- Your name/details: Anonymize
- Job URLs: Remove or replace with placeholder
- Contacts: Never include real names of people at companies

---

**Examples help others calibrate. Your real-world results are the best test of the Scanner.**
